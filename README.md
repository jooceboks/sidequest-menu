# Side Quest Menu
For people like me who want like to do a little bit of this and that. 

It's like a menu but for your day instead of food
You answer a few questions (mood, energy, if you're broke or not) and it spits out 4 things to do, appetizer, main, dessert + weekend special. 
no login, no saving, just decides for you so you're not doomscrolling for 20 min trying to figure out what to do in your free time
reroll whatever you don't vibe with, and you can save it and share with your friends :D
